# FoodForFun Atlas Vision

## Vision

To build the world's most trusted food knowledge network.

## Mission

Through food, understand people.
Through people, understand the world.

## Why Food?

Food is one of the few things every human shares.

Every dish is shaped by geography, climate, history, technology, and culture.

By understanding food, we begin to understand the people behind it.

## Our Belief

We are not building another food media platform.

We are building a new way to understand humanity through food.

## Long-term Goal

Preserve culinary knowledge.

Connect people across cultures.

Build an AI-powered global food knowledge network.
